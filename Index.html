About Us

Welcome to Lives Not Knives, where we champion a future free from the grip of violence. We're not just an organization; we're a movement committed to breaking the cycle of knife crime.

At Lives Not Knives, we believe in the power of every individual to choose a positive path. Through community engagement, education, and empowerment, we provide the tools and support needed to forge a life without knives.
